# Echo Cave Privacy Policy

Public policy URL: <https://echo-cave.suliemanhaidari.chatgpt.site/privacy>

Effective date: July 22, 2026

Echo Cave is designed to be an offline, private game. The app does not require an account and does not automatically collect or transmit gameplay, usage, diagnostic, or personal data. The developer does not sell or rent personal data or use it for advertising or tracking. Information you choose to send by support email is handled as described below.

## Data stored on your iPhone

Echo Cave stores game information locally on your device so that the game can work and remember your progress. This may include:

- Game progress, cave state, visited rooms, and move history.
- Inventory, coins, achievements, and journal entries.
- Daily Cave progress and results.
- Audio, haptic, presentation, and accessibility settings.
- Local technical state needed to restore or migrate a saved game.

This information stays on your iPhone. Echo Cave does not upload it to the developer or a server.

You can delete this locally stored information by deleting Echo Cave from your iPhone. A reset action inside the game may reset only the state described by its confirmation message; deleting the app removes all Echo Cave data stored by the app on that device. Because Echo Cave has no account or cloud service, deleted local progress cannot be recovered by the developer.

## Diagnostics

The App Store version may display on-device diagnostics to help identify audio, build, storage, or accessibility problems. These diagnostics remain on your device and are not transmitted automatically. The App Store version does not include the optional remote “Live Link” used during development of the earlier web prototype.

If you choose to copy diagnostic text and send it to support, the support section below applies.

## Sharing

If you choose Share Result for a Daily Cave, Echo Cave passes the text you see to the iOS share sheet. You decide whether to share it, which service or person receives it, and whether to cancel. Echo Cave does not receive or store the selected recipient, destination, or result after the share sheet takes over.

Any third-party app or service you choose in the share sheet handles the information under its own privacy policy.

## Support email

If you email support, the developer receives the information you voluntarily include, together with ordinary email metadata such as your email address and message time. This information is used only to respond, investigate the issue you reported, protect the app and its users, and meet legal obligations. Do not send passwords, financial information, health information, or other sensitive personal data.

Support correspondence is retained only as long as reasonably needed for those purposes and then deleted or de-identified, unless a longer period is required by law. You may request deletion of your support correspondence by emailing [vidalsulieman@gmail.com](mailto:vidalsulieman@gmail.com). The developer will honor a valid request unless retention is required for security, legal, or recordkeeping obligations.

Email delivery providers process messages as necessary to deliver and store email under their own terms and privacy policies.

## External links and Apple services

Echo Cave may open fixed links to its support, privacy, or accessibility pages when you request them. Your browser and the website host may process standard connection information under their own privacy policies. Echo Cave does not append your game progress or an app-specific user identifier to those links.

Apple separately processes information when you download the app, use the App Store or TestFlight, submit TestFlight feedback, or use other Apple services. That processing is governed by Apple's policies and settings, not this policy.

## Advertising, analytics, and tracking

Echo Cave contains no advertising SDK, third-party analytics SDK, cross-app tracking, or data broker integration. The developer does not use Echo Cave data to track you across apps or websites.

## Children

Echo Cave is not listed in the Made for Kids category. The app does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children. A parent or guardian who believes a child sent personal information in a support message can request its deletion using the contact below.

## Security

Keeping game data on your iPhone reduces the amount of information exposed to remote services. You are responsible for the security of your device, Apple Account, backups, and any information you choose to share outside Echo Cave.

## Changes to this policy

If Echo Cave's data practices change, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated before the changed version is released. Material changes will be identified by a new effective date and, when appropriate, an in-app notice.

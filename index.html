Stage 1 · find the exit
Your first cave is one straight path. Side passages are sealed. Listen to each doorway, follow it forward, reach the exit. Once you do, the cave opens up.

Tips

  • Lost? Open the cave map to see where you've been and where exits lie.
  • Stand still 4 seconds to enter Listen Mode — drips, wind, and door pings fade in.
  • The journal records lore as you discover it — press J or open it from the menu rotor.
  • If a clip cuts off, tap anywhere first — iOS only allows audio after a touch.
